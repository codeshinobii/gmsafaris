Explore Tanzania

Iconic Parks, Peaks, Lakes & Attractions

National Parks

Serengeti National Park

World-famous for the Great Migration, endless plains, and exceptional Big Cat sightings.

View More
Ngorongoro Conservation Area

Home to the stunning Ngorongoro Crater, a unique caldera teeming with wildlife, including the rare black rhino.

View More
Tarangire National Park

Famous for its massive elephant herds, ancient baobab trees, and diverse birdlife along the Tarangire River.

View More
Lake Manyara National Park

Known for its tree-climbing lions, large troops of baboons, flamingos, and stunning Rift Valley scenery.

View More
Arusha National Park

Home to Mount Meru, diverse landscapes, black-and-white colobus monkeys, and offers walking/canoeing safaris.

View More
Gombe Stream National Park

Famous for Jane Goodall's chimpanzee research; trek to see habituated chimps by Lake Tanganyika.

View More

Mountains & Highlands

Mount Kilimanjaro

Africa's highest peak and the world's tallest free-standing mountain. A challenging but rewarding trek.

View More
Mount Meru

Tanzania's second-highest peak, located within Arusha National Park. Offers great acclimatization and stunning views.

View More
Usambara Mountains

Biodiversity hotspot offering lush forests, cool air, hiking trails, and cultural encounters.

View More

Lakes

Lake Natron

A surreal soda lake, the primary breeding ground for Lesser Flamingos, with dramatic volcanic scenery.

View More
Lake Eyasi

A seasonal shallow salt lake, home to indigenous tribes like the Hadzabe hunter-gatherers and Datoga pastoralists.

View More
Lake Victoria

Africa's largest lake, offering fishing opportunities, bird watching, and access to islands like Rubondo.

View More

Other Attractions & Experiences

Zanzibar Archipelago

Famous for stunning beaches, turquoise waters, historic Stone Town (UNESCO site), spice farms, and rich culture.

View More
Materuni Waterfall & Coffee

Hike to a beautiful waterfall near Moshi, enjoy a hands-on coffee tour, and experience Chagga culture.

View More
Serval Wildlife Experience

Get up close with habituated servals, caracals, cheetahs, and more at this unique sanctuary near Kilimanjaro.
