Safari Overview

Maximize your Tanzanian holiday with the "Tanzanian Express," a perfect 2-day safari add-on from Zanzibar. This package conveniently includes return flights between Zanzibar and Arusha. On day one, fly to Arusha, explore the city, visit Serval Wildlife Reserve, and immerse yourself in local culture with our unique evening event. Stay overnight in a high-tier hotel.

Day two is dedicated to the magnificent Ngorongoro Crater, offering a world-class safari experience. After your game drive and a visit to a Maasai village, you'll be transferred back to Arusha for your return flight to Zanzibar. It's an efficient and exciting way to experience mainland Tanzania's highlights.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Fly Zanzibar to Arusha, Sightseeing & Culture

Zanzibar - Arusha

Morning flight from Zanzibar to Arusha (ARK). Met upon arrival and transfer for Arusha sightseeing and a visit to Serval Wildlife Reserve. Check into Njiro Legacy Hotel. In the evening, enjoy the #MyCultureOrYours experience with dinner and nightlife exploration.

Meals: Dinner (Cultural Event)

Accommodation: Njiro Legacy Hotel

2

Ngorongoro Crater, Maasai Visit & Fly to Zanzibar

Arusha - Ngorongoro - Arusha - Zanzibar

Early breakfast, then drive to Ngorongoro Crater for a morning game drive with picnic lunch. Afterwards, visit a Maasai village. Return drive to Arusha Airport (ARK) for your evening flight back to Zanzibar (ZNZ). Transfer from Zanzibar airport to your hotel is typically *not* included.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch (Picnic)

Important Notes

  • Price includes return flights Zanzibar-Arusha-Zanzibar & airport transfers in Arusha.
  • Price based on 2 people sharing. Solo/group rates available.
  • Accommodation is 1 night at Njiro Legacy Hotel (high-tier).
  • Flight times are subject to airline schedules. Soft bags recommended (luggage limits apply on small aircraft).
  • Recommended tipping: $10-$15 per person per day for the guide.
  • Travel insurance is mandatory.
  • Airport transfer in Zanzibar upon return is usually excluded, please check.
