Safari Overview

Experience the ultimate Tanzanian adventure with this comprehensive 6-day safari. Journey through diverse landscapes, from the elephant-rich Tarangire National Park to the vast plains of the Serengeti and the stunning Ngorongoro Crater. This itinerary blends incredible wildlife viewing with unique cultural encounters, including Arusha sightseeing, a Kilimanjaro foothills experience, a Maasai village visit, and interaction with rescued wildlife at Serval Wildlife Reserve.

Designed for those seeking an immersive experience, this tour combines iconic safari destinations with cultural highlights and comfortable stays in high-tier lodges or the Njiro Legacy Hotel in Arusha.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Arusha Sightseeing, Kili Foothills & Culture

Arusha & Materuni

Begin with Arusha sightseeing, then head to Mt. Kilimanjaro's foothills for a walk to Materuni Waterfall and a local coffee experience with lunch. Return to Arusha for an evening cultural experience (#MyCultureOrYours) and explore local nightlife.

Meals: Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Njiro Legacy Hotel (or similar)

2

Tarangire National Park Safari

Arusha to Tarangire

Depart after breakfast for Tarangire National Park, famous for its large elephant herds, baobab trees, and diverse birdlife. Enjoy a full-day game drive with a picnic lunch inside the park.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: High-Tier Safari Lodge near Tarangire

3

Transfer to Serengeti & Game Drive

Tarangire to Serengeti National Park

Drive towards the Serengeti, perhaps stopping at Olduvai Gorge (optional). Enter the vast Serengeti plains and enjoy an afternoon game drive en route to your accommodation, searching for the Big Five and other wildlife.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Serengeti High-Tier Safari Lodge/Camp

4

Full Day Serengeti Exploration

Serengeti National Park

Spend a full day exploring the Serengeti's diverse habitats. Depending on the season, your guide will focus on areas with high wildlife concentrations, possibly including the Great Migration. Optional Hot Air Balloon ride available at sunrise.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Serengeti High-Tier Safari Lodge/Camp

5

Ngorongoro Crater & Maasai Village

Serengeti to Ngorongoro & Karatu

After breakfast, drive to the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Descend into the Ngorongoro Crater for a spectacular game drive in this unique ecosystem. In the afternoon, visit a Maasai village to learn about their culture before heading to your lodge in Karatu.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: High-Tier Safari Lodge in Karatu

6

Serval Wildlife & Chemka Oasis - Departure

Karatu to Arusha/Kilimanjaro Airport (JRO)

Visit the Serval Wildlife Reserve for close encounters with rescued animals. Afterwards, relax and swim at the Chemka Hot Springs (offroad oasis). Transfer to Arusha or Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO) for your departure.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
